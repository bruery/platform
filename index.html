The Bruery - Business Application Platform (BAP)

The Bruery Platform offers developers a bootstrap template for developing business applications, by combining the tools they need. Built in PHP5, Symfony2 full-stack and extends Sonata Project Bundles, it seamlessly integrates into Bruery apps or works entirely standalone. Developing custom business applications has never been so easy.

Implementations

  • Bassoon - implementation of Bruery Platform.
  • Giggle - Learning Management System (LMS).
  • Fuzzy Spork - Human Resource Information System (HRIS).
  • Parakeet - Web based Payroll System.
  • Eureka - SSO for Bruery Platform.
  • Sniffle - Digital Asset Management System (DAMS).
